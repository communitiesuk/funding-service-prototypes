Use this service to check if your organisation is eligible and register to apply for the Safer social housing fund.

You will need to complete a short eligibility check before you can register your organisation and start your application.

Before you start

To complete the eligibility check and register your organisation, you will need:

  • to know your organisation type (for example, local authority, registered charity, or business)
  • your organisation's legal name and registered address
  • your Charity Commission number (if you are a registered charity)
  • your Companies House registration number (if you are a business)

If you pass the eligibility check and do not already have an account, you will be asked to provide an email address to set up your organisation's profile.

Start now

If you already have an account

If your organisation is already registered to use the Access Grant Funding service, you will be able to sign in and bypass the registration steps after confirming your eligibility.
