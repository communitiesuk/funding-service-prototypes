Background to this work

This journey was designed and developed following issues where grant recipients had submitted monitoring reports with mistakes and were then unable to make changes to the report.

Journeys

This journey enables a grant team policy member to find a submitted monitoring report and reopen it on behalf of the grant recipient so that they can correct and resubmit it. This was not tested formally with grant team policy members but is a small change that we expect to be intuitive and we will monitor usage. As part of the design and development, we discussed areas for potential future improvement around this functionality, listed under 'Next steps'.

Next steps

To monitor after release:

  • Consider adding other report statuses, e.g. 'Resubmitted' to help grant teams see submissions that have been reopened.
  • Consider whether the Submissions page should have a 'Back to top' link.
  • Monitor word counter on 'Why are you reopening this submission?' pageto see how many words users when reopening a submission.
  • Consider whether we should include role names as well as email addresses on 'Why are you reopening this submission?' page.
  • Consider whether submissions should show who submitted and certified the report.
  • Monitor whether the existing support desk options are sufficient to cover queries related to reopened submissions (we expect most requests to be submitted under 'Completing or submitting a report').
