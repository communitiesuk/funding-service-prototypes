Delivery planning

How is your programme progressing?

The Council has approved officers to undertake activities to work with key relevant stakeholders including the local MP to progress the themes agreed.

The paper was discussed in detail and the Council agreed to accept both funds. With regards to PiP Impact fund specifically an outline list of potential schemes was appended to the report including which themes they would support.

We have senior buy in from the Director of Growth who is responsible for our risk register and issues log. The officers on the programme have a biweekly meeting with the Director to update on progress, risks and issues.

We have started making progress on all themes and spend is on track with no concerns about under or overspend.

How have local organisations and communities been involved in the development and delivery of the programme?

We have engaged local organisations who have shaped the programme and its ways of working.

Our consultation and town hall, completed in February 2026 and March 2026 respectively, helped prioritise key areas of work, including:

* Blackpool Terrace

* Sea Gardens

* Lancashire View

* Gull Yards

In each of these areas, we're working with contacts representing the community. Our contacts are invited to give us regular feedback and updates.
